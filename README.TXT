Feature:
  -Record On-Fly: 
        Encode to mp3 from your Sound Card directly.
  -Encode File: 
        converter wav to mp3 file.
  -Live ShoutCast: 
        to transmit mp3 stream to another user in the net.
        (the another user need Winamp player to listen you)
         Hint: You can test your Live transmission with your 
         Winamp Player in the same PC. you must verify that 
         the Sound Card have full-duplex support (and how 
         the sample rate work this) or to select DirectSound 
         Plugin.
